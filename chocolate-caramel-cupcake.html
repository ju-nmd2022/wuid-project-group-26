Chocolate cupcake with caramel frosting

Imagine a juicy chocolate muffin with a caramel hide topped with a fluffy caramel frosting, garnished with lots of coarsely chopped chocolate and peanuts. Yes, you can hear for yourself how fantastic it sounds! And these cupcakes ARE amazingly delicious.

Prep time

45 min

Cook time

16 min

Quantity

12 muffins

Muffins Instructions

  1. Set the oven to 175 degrees and distribute cupcake forms in a cupcake tray.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan and pour into a bowl.
  3. Mix the melted butter with vanilla sugar, milk, water and eggs to a smooth batter.
  4. Then add wheat flour, cocoa, baking powder and caster sugar. Mix to a smooth batter.
  5. Divide the batter into 2/3 of the cupcake molds and bake in the middle of the oven for about 15 minutes. Allow to cool completely before garnishing.
Frosting Instructions
  1. Beat the butter and cream cheese together until fluffy before adding the dulche de leche. Whisk together to make a smooth and fluffy frosting.
  2. Make a hole in the center of each muffin and put some dulche de leche in each hole.
  3. Spread the frosting on each muffin and top generously with roughly chopped peanuts and chocolate.
  4. Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator.
Ingredients muffins
  • 75 g of butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 1.5 dl milk
  • 0.5 dl water
  • 1 egg
  • 3 dl wheat flour
  • 1 cup of cocoa
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2.5 dl granulated sugar
